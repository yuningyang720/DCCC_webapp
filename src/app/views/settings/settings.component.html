Settings

Please select a time that works best for you to participate in a brief reflection task. We recommend choosing a time during which you will be alone, and when it will be quiet enough for you to focus on your thoughts and feelings. Most people choose times between 7:00 PM and 11:00 PM. You will receive a notification at your selected time every day.

Part of this study involves checking in with you throughout the day to see how you are feeling. Please move the slider below to select the portion of the day during which you would like to receive these notifications. Keep in mind that the questions we ask are brief, and should not take more than a minute to complete.
